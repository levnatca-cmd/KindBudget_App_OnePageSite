FAQ

A few useful answers.

Still unsure about something? We are happy to help.

✉ support@kindbudget.app
Will KindBudget be free?+

Pricing and plan details will be confirmed before KindBudget is released on the App Store.

Do I need an account?+

Yes. An account is required so KindBudget can protect your data, sync the data supported by your plan and enable shared budgets. You can sign in with Apple, Google or email.

Can I manage a budget with someone else?+

Yes. Shared-budget owners can invite a partner or family member. Shared members only see the budgets they have been invited to.

Which currencies are supported?+

KindBudget supports USD, CAD, EUR and GBP. Each budget keeps its own currency.

Can KindBudget scan receipts?+

Yes. KindBudget uses on-device recognition to extract receipt details. Always review the detected amount before saving.

Which devices does it work on?+

KindBudget is designed for iPhone and requires iOS 18.2 or later. Its widget brings selected budget information to the Home Screen.

How do I delete my account and data?+

In the app, open Profile and choose Delete My Account. The two-step confirmation deletes your account and starts removal of associated data, subject to the shared-budget and retention details on the Delete Account page.

How can I contact support?+

Email support@kindbudget.app. Include your iPhone model, iOS version and app version so the team can help faster.
